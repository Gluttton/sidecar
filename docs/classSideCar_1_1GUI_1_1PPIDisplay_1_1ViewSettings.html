Detailed Description

Collection of settings that relate the the view shown in a PPIWidget.

Specifically, a view has horizontal and vertical offset for the radar's center, where zero offsets place the radar in the center of the screen; a zoom factor that magnifies the data; and a maximum visible range value that limits the samples used for the display.
