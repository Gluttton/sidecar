Detailed Description

Container for collections of SideCar messages, grouped by revolution.

As messages come in, they are added to live and pending Entry objects. When a complete revolution is detected, the pending Entry is placed at the front of the retained Entry queue, thus becoming the most-recent historical entry. The oldest Entry object is then popped off the back of the queue, keeping the queue a constant size, and the popped off Entry object becomes the new pending object.
